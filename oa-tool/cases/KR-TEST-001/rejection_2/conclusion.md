IV. Rejection reason 4: lack of clarity

1. Rejection reason 4-1

The examiner alleged that claim 7 is not clearly described (Patent Act Article 42(4)2) because the meaning of "first positioning reference value" is unclear. The examiner further stated that multiple terms are mixed throughout the specification, making the scope of protection unclear, and that the feature "dynamically adjusted according to a user movement pattern" lacks support in the specification.

To address the examiner's concern regarding the clarity of claim 7 and to ensure its full support by the description, we suggest amending claim 7.

Specifically, we propose to clarify the definition of "first positioning reference value" by incorporating its description into claim 7, and to delete the feature "dynamically adjusted according to a user movement pattern" as it is not sufficiently supported in the specification.
